What is Carpool?

        Carpooling is a system by which a person offers his or her private vehicle to one or more people who have similar destinations. A spirit solution for this problem is the usage of car-pooling systems, which support the task of vehicle (especially cars) sharing among users. Main motive is to provide a system which will enable easier sharing of vehicles, so that the number of vehicles in the roads can be decreased

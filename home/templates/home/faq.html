General questions

Is account registration required?

Account registration at QuickPool is mandatory as you will be placing or booking rides. This ensures a valid communication channel for all parties involved in any transactions.

Is my personal information secure?

We focus on storing data very securely without any unauthorized access. All the processing can be done by admin only.
Ride Hoster

What Placeride does?

It takes your ride details and make a database copy of it. When someone search for it our search manager places your ride to ride searcher.

If I select Online mode of payment how my payment is given to me?

The steps involved in this process are really simple. All you need to do is:
  • Register an account on paypal with the email id you registered with us
  • Activate your account
  • Select ONLINE mode of payment in the placeride
  • When ride acceptor pays the amount is credited to us and we than securely pay the money to you

Can I update my Ride Details

Yes, you can update ride until no user has booked the ride.
Still if you want, you can always cancel your ride and place the new one.
Ride Acceptor

When I can make online mode of payment?

The payment can be done once the ride date and time starts.

What are the payment options?

The best way to transfer funds is via Paypal. This secure platform ensures timely payments and a secure environment.
